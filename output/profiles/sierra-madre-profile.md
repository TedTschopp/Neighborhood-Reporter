Sierra Madre is a small foothill city in Los Angeles County, known as the  "Village of the Foothills" and "Wisteria City." Incorporated in 1907, it  features the world's largest flowering plant - a Chinese wisteria vine  measuring over one acre. The city maintains its own police and fire  departments and is part of the Pasadena Unified School District.
